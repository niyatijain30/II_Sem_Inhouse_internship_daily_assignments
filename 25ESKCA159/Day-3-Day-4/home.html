About SKIT

  • Swami Keshvanand Institute of Technology, Management & Gramothan was established in 2000 and is known as one of the academic excellence centers in Northern India.
  • The institute is affiliated to Rajasthan Technical University, Kota for graduate and postgraduate courses in engineering and management.
  • Located in Jaipur, SKIT focuses on practical orientation, industrial interaction and student-led activities for overall personality development.
  • Green landscaping, academic culture and modern learning facilities create a pleasant and dynamic campus environment for students.
